Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

The Campus Recruitment & Placement Cell (CR&PC) respects your privacy. This policy outlines how we handle data within our Certificate Verification Portal.

1. Zero-Knowledge Data Architecture

To ensure maximum security and student privacy, this system utilizes a strict zero-knowledge archive architecture. The backend database does not expose or store plain-text certificate details to the public internet.

Instead, queried IDs are cryptographically hashed on the server using PBKDF2 with a secret salt to safely locate matches. This prevents any reverse-engineering of student data.

2. Information Collection & Retention

We believe in data minimization. When you use this portal to verify a certificate:

  • No Query Logging: Queried certificate IDs are processed strictly in real-time memory to return validation results and are never saved, logged, or recorded permanently on the server.
  • No Tracking Cookies: We do not use third-party tracking cookies or analytics platforms to monitor your behavior.

3. Security & Anti-Abuse Measures

To protect the system against enumeration attacks, we monitor client IP addresses. However, this is done with utmost respect for privacy:

  • Client IP addresses are buffered temporarily in highly volatile serverless runtime memory.
  • This data is used solely to enforce our anti-abuse rate limits (a maximum of 10 requests per minute).
  • The IP data is instantly and automatically discarded after the 1-minute rolling window expires.

4. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer any data to outside parties. The verification results are provided strictly to the user initiating the query.

5. Contact Information

If you have any questions regarding this privacy policy or require official verification assistance, please contact the University Placement Cell.
